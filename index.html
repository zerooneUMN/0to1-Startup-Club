Our Thesis

The University of Minnesota is packed with world-class engineering and business talent.
Yet, too few take risks or build something that truly matters.
Through open meetings and our intensive three week student-ran startup accelerator, we're building something bigger:
A movement that will make the University of Minnesota one of the best entrepreneurship schools in the country.

About us

We're a community for entrepreneurs and visionaries building real-world startups. Our members are curious, ambitious, and ready to learn by doing. Whether you're exploring an idea, looking for a co-founder, or already building, 0-1 is where you'll find the people, resources, and momentum to take your startup from zero to one.

Public Meetings

Join us every Wednesday at 6:00 PM at the Carlson School of Management, Room 1-147. Our public meetings are open to anyone interested in entrepreneurship. We host guest speakers, run workshops, and discuss what it takes to go from zero to one. No experience needed -- just show up.

Stay Connected

Join our Slack workspace for meeting updates, resources, and to connect with other members between sessions.

Accelerator

Our 4-week intensive program is designed to take your idea from concept to a real, launched product. You'll work alongside a cohort of driven founders, receive mentorship from experienced entrepreneurs, and present your product at Demo Day.

What to Expect

Weekly workshops, 1-on-1 mentorship, peer feedback sessions, and a final Demo Day to showcase your product to investors and the campus community.

Dinner Series

Our exclusive dinner events connect ambitious students with local founders and startup leaders in an intimate setting. Share a meal, hear real stories, and build meaningful relationships with people who've been where you want to go.

How It Works

Limited seats per dinner to keep conversations personal. Invitations go out through our Slack -- join the community and stay tuned for the next event.
